# 🌸 ChurchCRM 7.4.1 — The "Secure Access & Simplified Setup" Update

**Release Date**: July 6, 2026

**Theme**: Clearer Permissions, Confident Administration, and Deeper Privacy

Welcome to **ChurchCRM 7.4.1**! Following our major 7.4.0 milestone, this release focuses directly on making user management and data privacy more intuitive. We have carried out a comprehensive audit of our security permissions, ensuring that managing your staff, volunteers, and congregation data is less confusing and significantly more secure.

---

## 🔑 Simplified & Hardened User Administration

Setting up new users and managing what they can see has been completely streamlined to eliminate guesswork.

* **Clarser Permission Labels:** We have completely rewritten the labels on the User Editor page. Instead of vague technical terms, permission settings are now explicitly labeled so you know exactly what access you are granting.
* **Smart Dashboard Buttons:** Action buttons like "Add Person" or "Edit Family" are now intelligently hidden if a user doesn't have the permission to use them. This removes interface clutter and prevents accidental clicks that end in "Access Denied" screens.
* **Exclusive "Edit-Self" Mode:** The "EditSelf" feature has been isolated into an exclusive permission mode. To keep your database airtight out of the box, all newly created accounts now default to `EditSelf = 0` (disabled).
* **Flawless User Deletion:** Fixed a long-standing database issue where attempting to completely delete a staff or volunteer account would fail if that user had custom configurations saved. Deletions now process cleanly.

---

## 🛡️ Intuitive Privacy & Access Guardrails

We have refined our access boundaries so everyday users have the information they need to do their jobs without exposing sensitive underlying data.

* **Explicit Default Access:** Authenticated church staff and volunteers now explicitly have basic read-only access to necessary household profiles, family navigation, and avatars by default, preventing unexpected visual gaps during daily operations.
* **Airtight Notes Protection:** We have strictly reinforced our read/write policies for member timelines. Pastoral and administrative notes are fiercely guarded behind designated permission scopes.
* **URL Security Patching:** We patched hidden security vulnerabilities (such as route parameter manipulation and unescaped form links in the Pledge Editor) to prevent malicious actors from navigating past your server's intended boundaries.

---

## 🗺️ New Dedicated Regional & Language Hub

Managing a diverse congregation just got easier with a brand-new administrative control center.

* **Centralized Localization Hub:** We have introduced a dedicated admin section at `admin/system/localization`. From here, you can easily manage time zones, text formats, and language rules globally without touching a line of code.
* **Public Calendar Fixes:** The public-facing church calendar now fully honors your local translation settings down to the last detail.

---

## 🇭🇷 Welcome Croatian Support!

Our global platform expands its reach to support even more ministries:

* **Official Croatian (hr) Support:** We are incredibly excited to welcome Croatian to our official language matrix, pre-configured with translation logic optimized for traditional denominations.
* **A Massive Global Project:** We have pushed complete translation sweeps across **all 42 supported languages** to ensure all new user security prompts and admin configurations speak your team's native language.

---

## 🪲 Other Quality-of-Life Wins

* **Interactive Filter Resetting:** Fixed an annoying bug where clicking the "Clear Filter" button would clear out checkboxes but fail to actually reset the data list underneath. Filters now clear instantly.
* **Smarter Event Math:** The dashboard's monthly event attendance averages are now computed cleanly in-memory, resolving a long-standing mathematical hiccup.

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminder

* **Server Requirement:** This version continues to **require PHP 8.4**.
* **Guided Upgrade:** Our automated migration wizard is fully prepared to transition your site smoothly from 7.4.0 to 7.4.1.
