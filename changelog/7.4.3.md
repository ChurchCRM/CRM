# 🌸 ChurchCRM 7.4.3 — The "Digital Housekeeping" Release

**Release Date**: July 17, 2026

**Theme**: Better Folder Privacy, Lean User Settings, and Snappy Dashboards

**Version 7.4.3** is all about streamlining the system behind the scenes. We've spent time tightening up data folders, removing redundant options from your setup pages, and upgrading old code so your daily ministry tools load faster and run more predictably.

---

## 🔒 Better Privacy for Family Photos

We’ve given your uploaded files a dedicated privacy upgrade to ensure your congregation’s information stays private.

* **Locked Profile Media:** Your member and family photos are now completely private. The system now actively blocks unauthenticated web requests from trying to look into your photo folders, ensuring that only logged-in staff and volunteers can see your directory photos.

---

## 🔑 Leaner Settings & Simplified Admin Menus

We are continuing our mission to clear out the clutter on your settings pages so managing your team is a breeze.

* **Removed Redundant Options:** We have completely deleted the individual, per-user checkboxes for "Create Directory" and "Export CSV." Instead of making you toggle these settings for every single volunteer, these capabilities are now handled automatically by overall system permission levels—giving you a cleaner, faster user setup screen.
* **Modernized User Editor:** The backend panel where you create and modify user accounts has been completely rebuilt. It now loads faster, handles errors more smoothly, and operates seamlessly with modern browsers.

---

## 💼 Snappy Fundraiser Pages

* **Smoother Page Loads:** The underlying code running your **Fundraiser Pages** has received a complete modernization (`/fundraiser/`). This gives you quick page transitions and ensures your fundraising tools look and feel exactly like the rest of your primary church dashboards.

---

## 🌍 Continual Global Polish

* **Perfected Localizations:** Our translation teams have double-checked all **42 supported languages** to ensure that all new menu options and administrative alerts are written in natural, clear language for your local church staff.

---

## ⚠️ Upgrade Reminder

* **Guided Upgrade:** Our automated upgrade wizard is standing by to safely update your site from 7.4.2 directly to 7.4.3 with a single click.
